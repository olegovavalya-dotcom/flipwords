Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

FlipWords is a free English learning tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle data.

1. Who We Are

FlipWords is a free online English vocabulary learning application at flipwords-ua.netlify.app, operated by an independent developer in Ukraine. Contact: olegovavalya@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

  • No account required — use FlipWords without registering or sharing personal information.
  • No personal data storage — we do not store names, emails, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Session data — game progress is stored temporarily in your browser only and disappears when you close the tab.
  • Anonymized analytics — we may collect anonymous usage data with no personal identifiers.

3. Cookies

FlipWords itself does not use tracking cookies. However third-party services may:

  • Google AdSense — may use cookies to show personalized ads. Opt out at Google Ad Settings.
  • Google Fonts — may log your IP address per Google's privacy policy.

4. Advertising

FlipWords displays ads via Google AdSense. Ad networks may collect your approximate location, device type, and browsing behavior to show relevant advertisements. This data is processed per their own privacy policies.

5. Children's Privacy

FlipWords is suitable for all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Your Rights

Since we store no personal data there is nothing to access or delete. You may opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings or contact us: olegovavalya@gmail.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy and will update the "Last updated" date accordingly. Continued use of FlipWords means acceptance of any changes.

8. Contact Us

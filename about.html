What is FlipWords?

FlipWords is a free English vocabulary learning application built for Ukrainian speakers and anyone who wants to improve their English through daily practice. The app uses the classic flashcard method, one of the most research-backed ways to memorize new words quickly.

Each flashcard shows you an English word on the front. You try to recall the meaning, then flip the card to see the Ukrainian translation and an example sentence in context. You then tell the app whether you knew the word or need more practice — helping you track your progress in real time.

Our goal is simple: make English vocabulary learning free, fast, and fun. No subscriptions, no accounts, no setup — just open the site and start learning.

Why Flashcards Work

Flashcards are one of the oldest and most effective language learning tools. They are based on active recall — instead of passively reading word lists, you actively try to remember the meaning before revealing the answer. This forces your brain to work harder, creating stronger and longer-lasting memory traces.

Research consistently shows that active recall outperforms passive study by a significant margin. Students who use flashcards regularly retain vocabulary 2 to 3 times longer compared to those who simply read or highlight text.

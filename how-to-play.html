How to Play

FlipWords is super easy to get started. Follow these simple steps and you will be learning English vocabulary in seconds.

1

Choose a Category

At the top of the game you will see four category buttons: Basics, Business, Travel, and Emotions. Tap the category that matches what you want to practice today. Each category contains 10 carefully selected words.

2

Read the English Word

A flashcard appears with an English word and an emoji. Look at the word carefully and try to remember what it means in Ukrainian. Take a moment to think before moving on.

3

Flip the Card

When ready, tap the card or press "Reveal answer". The card flips with a smooth animation to show you the Ukrainian translation and a real example sentence so you can see how the word is used in context.

4

Rate Your Answer

Two buttons appear after the flip. Press "Know it!" if you already knew the word. Press "Learn more" if the word was new or you forgot it. Be honest with yourself for the best learning results.

5

Track Your Progress

At the top you can see live stats: how many cards you got right, the current card number, and how many need more practice. A progress bar shows how far through the deck you are.

6

See Your Results

After finishing all 10 cards you see a result screen with your score as a percentage. Scored 80% or higher? Excellent! Below 50%? Press "Play again" to repeat the deck and improve.

Tips for Faster Learning

  • 🌅
    Practice daily — even 5 minutes a day is enough. Consistency beats long study sessions.
  • 🗣️
    Say words out loud — speaking activates different memory pathways and improves retention significantly.
  • 📝
    Write new words down — if a word surprises you, write it in a notebook. Writing reinforces memory.
  • 🔁
    Repeat the deck — if your score was below 70%, play the same category again immediately.
  • 🎯
    Focus on one category per day — master one topic at a time to build strong vocabulary clusters.
  • 📖
    Use example sentences — read the sentence on the back of each card to understand the word in context.
